Pipeline: vite demo server → Playwright headless Chromium (1440x900, light+dark, scrolling segments of `.preview-wrap`) → Gemini designer critique → Claude agent pixel-crop verification. Findings marked unconfirmed did not survive visual verification but were kept as possible nitpicks.
**Totals: 3 high / 13 medium / 20 low confirmed across 12 templates.**


## ⚠ Capture flake recurrence (retractions inside)

The blank-first-paint flake recurred this cycle despite the DOM-content guard: 7 of 12 templates captured a blank light-mode preview on the first pass. Four agents recaptured and cleared it themselves; three reported it as a confirmed defect (team-scheduler, budget-tracker, pricing-plans) and were disproven by post-hoc recapture — those findings are retracted below. Root cause refined: template text is in the DOM while pixels are still blank (entrance animation or late style application under concurrent-capture CPU load). The capture script now retries until the preview region screenshot exceeds a minimum compressed size, a mechanism-agnostic "actual pixels present" check.
