## ⚠ Cross-cutting: intermittent blank first paint

Four templates (read-later-library, ops-wall-display, habit-streak-tracker, drag-rank-list) captured as a completely blank preview card in one color scheme on first load, then rendered fully on recapture. The script now waits for preview content. No code-level investigation needed. The per-template "blank render" findings below were downgraded accordingly.
